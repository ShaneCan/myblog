I am a research assistant at the [Chair of Software Technology (ST group)](https://tu-dresden.de/ing/informatik/smt/st), Technische Universität Dresden, where I am pursuing my PhD under the guidance of [Prof. Uwe Aßmann](http://www1.inf.tu-dresden.de/~ua1/). I am also a member of [RTG 2947 "AirMetro"](https://tu-dresden.de/bu/verkehr/airmetro/rtg-2947-ueberblick), led by [Prof. Hartmut Fricke](https://tu-dresden.de/bu/verkehr/ila/ifl/die-professur/staff/Hartmut_Fricke).

My research focuses on **formal model-based digital twins** for Advanced Air Mobility (AAM) operations, including their integration with computer vision for perception and situational awareness, and robotics for autonomous coordination at airport surfaces.
