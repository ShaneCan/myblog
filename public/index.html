About

I am a research assistant at the Chair of Software Technology (ST group), Technische Universität Dresden, where I am pursuing my PhD under the guidance of Prof. Uwe Aßmann. I am also a member of the project RTG 2947 „AirMetro“, leading by Prof. Hartmut Fricke. Currently, my research interests focus on formal model–based digital twins, emphasizing their integration into Advanced Air Mobility (AAM) operations. I am also interested in computer vision for perception and situational awareness, as well as robotics for autonomous operation and coordination within airport surface.
