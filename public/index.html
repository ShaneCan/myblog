About

Tianxiong Zhang

PhD Researcher

Technische Universität Dresden

I am a research assistant at the Chair of Software Technology (ST group), Technische Universität Dresden, where I am pursuing my PhD under the guidance of Prof. Uwe Aßmann. I am also a member of RTG 2947 “AirMetro”, led by Prof. Hartmut Fricke.

My research focuses on formal model-based digital twins for Advanced Air Mobility (AAM) operations, including their integration with computer vision for perception and situational awareness, and robotics for autonomous coordination at airport surfaces.
